About

I work with messy, real-world fund and market data and turn it into signals that people can actually use. I’m most interested in building tools that clarify risk early, before it becomes a decision problem.

My background is quantitative (math and operations research), and I’m drawn to work at the intersection of data engineering, portfolio intuition, and practical decision support.

Current Role

I’m currently a Cross-Asset Investment Risk Analyst at Morgan Stanley in New York, working on liquidity monitoring, counterparty exposure, and derivatives leverage across multi-asset fund portfolios.
